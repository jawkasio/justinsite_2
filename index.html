About Us

Our psychotherapists take a compassionate and integrative approach in which clients learn to access the vast power of their inner resources.

Whether you are struggling with a major life transition, a challenging relationship, significant loss, or the pain of trauma, you can begin to come to terms with your present reality and make meaningful changes to enhance your well-being and personal growth.

Our Psychotherapists

Justin Appler (He/Him)

Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying)

Clinical Director and Founder

Supporting: Youth (12+), Individuals, Couples and Families

Languages: English

Justin is passionate about journeying alongside his clients and knows first-hand the healing potential of therapy. With empathy, compassion, and a deep appreciation for spirituality, he supports folks navigating uncertainty, life transitions, and emotional challenges.

His approach to therapy is grounded in Accelerated Experiential Dynamic Psychotherapy (AEDP). This is a deeply relational trauma-informed modality which harnesses the power of the body-mind to heal by undoing aloneness through the therapeutic relationship.

Justin specializes in:

  • Grief
  • Trauma
  • Anxiety
  • Stress
  • Depression
  • Life transitions
  • Relationship difficulties
  • Spirituality and religion
  • Death and dying
  • Mindfulness
  • 2SLGBTQIA+

Rachel Alencar (Sher/Her)

Registered Psychotherapist (Qualifying)

Supporting: Youth (12+), Individuals, Couples and Families

Languages: English and Portuguese

Rachel supports individuals navigating neurodivergence, gender-based violence, newcomer adjustment, and trauma-related concerns, including suicidality and PTSD, offering compassionate and informed care across diverse communities.

She is here to support folks in strengthening their relationships, navigating emotions with greater ease, and developing practical skills to feel grounded in their daily lives. Her approach brings together evidence-based modalities to help clients reconnect with themselves and their strengths, break unhelpful patterns, and make meaningful, empowered choices that align with their values.

Rachel specializes in:

  • Depression
  • PTSD
